## Digital Marketing & E-Business Strategies (3040003361)

## Pre-requisite

- Basic knowledge of computers, the internet, and fundamental business concepts. Familiarity with social media platforms and online communication will be an added advantage.

## Course Objective

- To provide students with a comprehensive understanding of digital marketing concepts, tools, e-business strategies, and emerging trends to effectively operate in the digital business environment.

## Teaching Scheme

| Lecture | Theory | Practical | Hours | Credit |
| ------- | ------ | --------- | ----- | ------ |
| 2       | 0      | 0         | 2     | 2      |

## Unit-01 Fundamentals of Digital Marketing & E-Business (29%)

- Introduction to Digital Marketing: Definition, evolution, and scope
- Traditional Marketing vs. Digital Marketing
- Core concepts of E-Business: Definition, models, and importance
- Types of E-Business models: B2B, B2C, C2C, C2B
- Advantages, limitations, and challenges of digital marketing and e-business, Overview of digital customer behavior

## Unit-02 Digital Marketing Tools & Techniques (29%)

- Search Engine Optimization (SEO): On-page & off-page techniques
- Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and Pay-Per-Click (PPC) campaigns
- Social Media Marketing (SMM): Platforms, strategies, and engagement
- Content Marketing: Creating value-driven content, Email Marketing and Affiliate Marketing basics
- Web analytics: Google Analytics & measuring campaign performance

## Unit-03 E-Business Strategies & Applications (21%)

- Designing effective e-business strategies
- Online business models and revenue generation methods
- E-payment systems: Types, security issues, and emerging trends
- E-CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and its role in business growth
- Mobile commerce (M-commerce) and its applications
- Case studies of successful e-business strategies (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.)

## Unit-04 Emerging Trends & Future of Digital Business (21%)

- Artificial Intelligence (AI) and automation in digital marketing
- Role of Big Data and Analytics in decision making
- Cloud computing and digital business transformation
- Ethical and legal issues in digital marketing & e-business
- Cybersecurity challenges in e-business
- Future trends: Voice commerce, AR/VR in marketing, Metaverse, and Blockchain in e-business
